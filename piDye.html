Pi Dye

iPhone app for memorizing color-coded digits of pi

see on App Store

the more senses, the better


Inspiration

This app allows you to see pi from the eyes of a synesthete. People with synesthesia often see letters and numbers in color (the number 3 may always appear yellow, the letter A always looks red, etc.). Synesthetes are known for their incredible memorization power due to increased neurological associations, many of which are linked to color. As a grapheme-color synesthete myself, I chose this fun side project to show all the non-synesthetes out there how helpful - and fun - these associations are for memorizing.
