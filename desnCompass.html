DESN Compass

a full, responsive web guide to Notre Dame's own West Lake Hall

see for yourself

Final Product

This responsive website was a group effort of several designers in Notre Dame's third Visual Communication Class. Together, we identified our clients' needs, collected data beneficial to the design community, compiled original photography, and adapted an html5 template that best suited our requirements. Its functionality includes easy access to door codes in the design building, quick-info about our clients' favorite local food spots, the ability to submit suggestions/complaints directly to the design department faculty, and more!
