Bio

With a degree in Computer Science from Notre Dame, Madelyn is comfortable in several computer languages, preferring JavaScript, Python, and C++. But that's not all - she runs, knits, and plays the trumpet and violin.

She has contributed to seven plus iPhone apps, five of which were individual projects, and four of which are currently available on the App Store. She's worked on several web apps, including a Python Flask app for AbbVie's antibody engineering group. Her group's hackathon hack for public speaking was recognized at the Major League Hacking's Spring Finale for its impressive integration of several API's and elegant front-end. She enjoys tackling all kinds of coding projects and embraces a good challenge.

Madelyn is passionate about computer science education. For her senior thesis, she created a web app for providing resources to Americans of all ages interested in pursuing computer science education. In her free time, she volunteers as a coding instructor at a local elementary school. She is also involved with the South Bend Code School where she enjoys developing websites and mobile apps for the community, as well as teaching basic web development to underserved youth.
