Bio

With a degree in Computer Science from Notre Dame, Madelyn is comfortable in several computer languages, preferring JavaScript, Python, and C++. But that's not all - she runs, knits, and plays the trumpet and violin. She has contributed to seven plus iPhone apps, five of which were individual projects, and four of which are currently available on the App Store. She's worked on several web apps, creating a Python Flask app from scratch for AbbVie's antibody engineering group, and being recognized at collegiate hackathons. She's also embellished the Notre Dame Trumpet Section's website with D3 and custom design. Madelyn is passionate about computer science education. For her senior thesis, she created a web app for providing resources to Americans of all ages interested in pursuing computer science education. In her free time, she volunteers as a coding instructor at a local elementary school, and is involved at The South Bend Code School.
