Freddie the Fly

can you save Freddie from a miserable death-by-frog?

see for yourself





Freddie the fly just wants to fly. What started as a six-touch-point project soon escalated to a web game of infinite tactility. This game, while designed for iPad useres, can adapt to a variety of screen sizes. The premise is simple: don't let the fly die. The fly jumps with every click, so it's up to the user to adapt to the gravity and jump at the perfect moment.
